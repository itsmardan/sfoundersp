Saudi Donor Award

Celebrates those who champion philanthropy and social investment. Saudi Donors drive sustainable development and prosperity by empowering underserved communities. Their generosity transforms lives and inspires others to prioritise giving as a fundamental pillar of progress.

The Saudi Donor Award honours individuals and organisations whose philanthropic contributions create lasting social impact. These donors lead initiatives that promote education, healthcare, economic opportunity, and community well-being, paving the way for equitable growth across Saudi Arabia.

Membership in SaudiFounders.org connects donors with opportunities to expand their philanthropic reach through impact-driven programmes, collaborations, and data-backed strategies for social transformation.

Our vision is to cultivate a culture of generosity that strengthens communities, fosters economic stability, and builds sustainable opportunities for future generations. We highlight the stories and successes of Saudi philanthropists to inspire more meaningful contributions across society.
