Saudi Founders Awards

Discover the individuals and organisations shaping the future of Saudi Arabia’s industries, communities, and economy through visionary leadership, innovation, and social impact.

Saudi Star Award

Recognises individuals for outstanding leadership and achievements across industries. Honours those who push boundaries, drive innovation, and inspire positive change. Saudi Star winners set benchmarks through their vision and impact on both business and society.

Growth Icon Award

Celebrates leaders driving exceptional organisational growth. Awardees demonstrate strategic foresight, transforming enterprises into dynamic success stories. Their achievements reflect perseverance, agility, and bold decision-making, making them icons of business transformation.

Social Hero Award

Rewards those making significant social contributions. Champions of public welfare through charitable efforts, advocacy, and community-building. Their work creates ripples of transformation, improving lives and fostering unity. Social Heroes inspire new generations to commit to making the world better for all.

Future Icon Award

Acknowledges innovators shaping tomorrow’s industries. Pioneers of cutting-edge technologies, sustainable initiatives, and future-focused enterprises. Future Icons are early adopters who envision and lead the evolution of markets, inspiring others to embrace forward-thinking progress.

Legacy Leader Award

Honours seasoned leaders whose careers leave a lasting impact. Recognised for their wisdom, resilience, and consistent success across decades. Legacy Leaders act as mentors and role models, passing down knowledge and values that shape the foundations of industry and society.

Saudi Donor Award

Celebrates those committed to philanthropy and social investment. By empowering underserved communities, Saudi Donors drive sustainable development and prosperity. Their generosity transforms futures, encouraging others to prioritise giving as a cornerstone of progress.
