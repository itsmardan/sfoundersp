Open Invite

Saudi Founders Open Invite

On 1st May 2025, 100 Saudi founders will be celebrated—visionaries whose stories of ambition, resilience, and impact will inspire generations. This initiative invites you to join a movement that honours those shaping Saudi Arabia’s future through innovation, leadership, and philanthropy.

Nominate yourself or someone deserving to be featured at saudifounders.org.

Impact

Every founder has a story—a journey of challenges, triumphs, and bold visions. By sharing your story, you:

  • Inspire others to pursue success.
  • Contribute to Saudi Arabia’s legacy of innovation.
  • Celebrate your achievements on a national and global platform.

This is more than an award. It is a recognition of your journey as part of the nation’s progress.

Recognition

Our awards platform highlights your story through membership, interviews, and biographies. Selected founders receive:

  • A certificate and membership on SaudiFounders.org.
  • An online profile for national and international visibility.
  • Media coverage to amplify your impact.

Read more about each award category at Awards Overview.

Awards

We recognise visionaries and changemakers across various categories. From business innovators to social contributors, the awards showcase leaders shaping tomorrow.

Learn about all categories, including:

  • Saudi Star Award
  • Growth Icon Award
  • Social Hero Award
  • Future Icon Award
  • Legacy Leader Award
  • Saudi Donor Award

Explore full details at Awards Overview.

Nomination

Nominating is simple:

  1. Visit saudifounders.org/nominate.
  2. Share your story, achievements, and impact.
  3. Submit your nomination before 31/03/2025.

Take part in this historic initiative and become a beacon of inspiration for generations to come.

History

On 1st May 2025, the first 100 founders will set a benchmark of excellence and leadership. Their stories will become a permanent source of inspiration for others to rise and achieve greatness.

This is your opportunity to:

  • Immortalise your journey.
  • Inspire the next generation of leaders.
  • Join a community of visionaries dedicated to building Saudi Arabia’s future.

Nominate now and let your story shine.
